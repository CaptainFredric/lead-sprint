# Niche Campaigns

## Purpose

Use niche campaigns to avoid generic outreach. The first email should make the owner feel you looked at their business model, not just their homepage.

## Campaign Pattern

For each niche, define:

- Typical job value
- Best buyer
- First offer
- Opening angle
- Lead leaks to inspect
- Objection handle

Then use the live site's niche campaign builder to apply that angle to the audit console.

## Home Services

Best buyer: owner/operator

Opening angle: make emergency quote and call paths easier to find from mobile.

Lead leaks:

- No clear call or quote action above the fold
- Mobile layout is hard to scan
- Calls and forms are not tracked

## Health And Wellness

Best buyer: practice owner

Opening angle: turn trust, reviews, and appointment intent into a clearer booking path.

Lead leaks:

- Reviews are not near the offer
- Appointment form is buried
- Mobile layout feels dated

## Legal Practices

Best buyer: managing partner

Opening angle: clarify the intake path for urgent, high-value cases.

Lead leaks:

- Call action is unclear
- Proof and credibility are too far down the page
- Intake events are not tracked

## Auto Services

Best buyer: shop owner

Opening angle: make quote requests, service menus, and proof easier to scan on mobile.

Lead leaks:

- Mobile layout is hard to scan
- Quote form asks too much
- Reviews are not near the service offer

## Wedding And Events

Best buyer: founder or booking manager

Opening angle: turn visual interest into a faster inquiry path with proof and availability cues.

Lead leaks:

- Portfolio is visible but inquiry path is unclear
- Form is buried
- Reviews and venue proof are separated from the offer

## Professional Services

Best buyer: principal or owner

Opening angle: make expertise, fit, and consultation requests easier to understand.

Lead leaks:

- Offer is unclear
- Consultation path is hidden
- Calls and forms are not tracked

## Rule

If you cannot name the niche-specific lead leak in one sentence, do not send the email yet.
